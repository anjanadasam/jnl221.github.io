HTML tags you should know

This is called a p tag. P stands for 'paragraph'. In here, you'd put text to form a paragraph. Don't forget to close the p tag!

This is also a paragraph. However, this paragraph contains an a tag, which allows you to insert a link. In this paragraph, I want to show you an article on The Daily Orange about a poet from Ghana. Pay attention to the structure of an a tag and what goes inside of it.
